About This Project:

This project was created using a graphical library/framework called Isomer.js. You can view the documentation for this library by looking under the "Links" section or by clicking the "Source" tab on the navigation menu at the top of the screen. This project simulates generative three dimensional art by randomly generating isometric structures with random colors or a chosen pallete of colors. Users can also download generated structures by clicking the "Download" tab on the navigation menu.
